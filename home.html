What is Digital Alkansiya?

Digital Alkansiya is Create Foundation's online giving platform, under the You Can Create Campaign — a fundraising movement that has been driving digital readiness across the Philippines since 2025.

It primarily funds Project CLIMB (Create Learning in Mountain Barangays), a digital readiness initiative under our Create Library program focusing in upland communities. We believe that technology in many of these areas is like a second language. And to be able to speak a second language, basic literacy and numeracy skills are primarily required.

What started as simple physical cans passed from hand to hand has grown into the Digital Alkansiya — a more sustainable, accessible, and modern way to give.
